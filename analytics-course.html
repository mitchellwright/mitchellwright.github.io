Do you know exactly how to use Google Analytics to make important decisions about your blog?

If you're like most bloggers, you know how to do the basics in Google Analytics. You can check your pageviews, see which posts are the most popular, and maybe you even know how to see where your traffic is coming from.

You do your best to create good content, get it posted on different social media sites, increase your rankings on Google, and get featured by other websites, but it's hard to know what is working the best, or where to spend your time for the biggest returns.

The big problem with Google Analytics...

...is that so few people really understand how to use it! And that's a shame because there is so much amazing information in Google Analytics that can really change the way you work on your blog.

Let's stop for a moment and think about how your blog would be different if you could know:

  • Exactly how people are coming to your blog
  • What people are doing once they get to your blog
  • Which pages had huge opportunities to drive more traffic from Google search

All of this data and more is right there in Google Analytics, waiting for you! All you have to do is know a few key things about how to properly use the tool.
